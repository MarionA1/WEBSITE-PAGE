About Coffee Bliss Haven

At Coffee Bliss Haven, we believe that coffee is not just a drink—it’s an experience. From the moment you step into our cozy, tranquil space, you’ll be immersed in the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the warmth of genuine hospitality. It’s a place where the art of coffee meets the heart of community.

Our journey began with a simple but profound vision: to create a haven for coffee lovers—a space where you can slow down, savor the moment, and indulge in the finest handpicked beans, expertly crafted to perfection.

Each cup tells a story of passion, dedication, and expertise. Whether you're enjoying a smooth espresso or a velvety cappuccino, we aim to elevate your coffee experience to new heights. It's about more than just great coffee; it's about bringing people together over a shared love for life's simple pleasures.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to craft the finest coffee experiences for our community. Every cup we serve is a promise of quality, warmth, and a passion for perfection. We are dedicated to supporting sustainable farming practices, working closely with local farmers to bring you the best beans the world has to offer.

Our Values

  • Quality: We are committed to using only the finest ingredients, ensuring each cup of coffee is rich, flavorful, and unforgettable.
  • Community: We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere for everyone—whether you're here for a quiet escape or a lively gathering with friends.
  • Sustainability: Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond just great coffee. We support ethical sourcing and environmentally responsible practices to make a positive impact on the world.

Our Signature Menu

Crafted with passion, our menu features an array of delicious options for every coffee lover. Explore our offerings below:

Kenyan AA Coffee

Description: The finest Kenyan coffee beans, known for their full-bodied flavor and aroma. Perfect for any coffee lover who enjoys a rich and bold cup of coffee.

Ingredients: Premium Kenyan AA beans, water, sugar (optional), milk (optional).

Price: Ksh 300

Recommended Pairing: A perfect match with our freshly baked croissants or savory sandwiches.

Black Coffee

Description: Straight from the finest Kenyan beans, this coffee is bold, aromatic, and pure. Ideal for those who appreciate a strong, no-nonsense cup of coffee.

Ingredients: 100% Kenyan Arabica beans, water.

Price: Ksh 250

Recommended Pairing: Pairs wonderfully with a slice of our banana bread or a savory pastry.

Hot Chocolate

Description: A creamy, rich blend of high-quality cocoa beans and milk. This indulgent treat is perfect for anyone who loves a sweet, chocolatey experience.

Ingredients: Cocoa powder, sugar, milk, water.

Price: Ksh 150

Recommended Pairing: Best enjoyed with a piece of our chocolate cake or a crisp biscuit.

Mandazi

Description: A traditional East African sweet pastry, deep-fried to a golden crisp. Soft and fluffy on the inside, this treat is perfect with any hot drink.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, yeast, water, oil.

Price: Ksh 40

Recommended Pairing: Pairs wonderfully with a cup of Kenyan AA coffee or a refreshing iced tea.

Beef Samosas

Description: Savory and crispy, filled with spiced beef, onions, and herbs. A great option for those looking for something savory alongside their coffee.

Ingredients: Beef, onions, garlic, spices, pastry.

Price: Ksh 50

Recommended Pairing: Goes perfectly with a cup of black coffee or hot chocolate for a balanced snack.

Chapati

Description: Soft and slightly crispy flatbread, made from simple ingredients and served warm. A popular choice for a quick snack or accompaniment to your meal.

Ingredients: Flour, water, salt, oil.

Price: Ksh 40

Recommended Pairing: A great snack with our Kenyan AA Coffee or as a side with a hearty stew.
